About Us

AKAF Company is a leading provider of specialized manpower, engineering support, and industrial services, delivering reliable workforce solutions to major industries across Saudi Arabia.

With over 14 years of proven experience, we supply certified professionals, skilled technicians, and technical teams to support Oil & Gas, Petrochemical, Construction, Infrastructure, and large-scale industrial projects.

Driven by a dedication to safety, quality, and operational excellence, we partner with clients to ensure seamless project execution, enhanced productivity, and sustained long-term performance.
